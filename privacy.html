Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At CS Software, your privacy is our top priority. We build our apps with the philosophy of collecting no personal data, or only the absolute minimum data needed for the app to function. We believe everyone deserves software that respects their privacy.

Information We Collect

Our apps are designed to work without collecting personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personal identifiers.
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not sell, share, or trade any user data with third parties.
  • If an app requires minimal data to function (e.g., local preferences), that data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

Some of our apps or our website may use third-party services such as:

  • Google Analytics — to understand general website traffic and usage patterns. This may collect anonymized, non-personal data such as browser type, device type, and pages visited. No personally identifiable information is collected.
  • Google AdSense — to display ads on our website. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • App Store / Google Play — when you download our apps, the respective platform's privacy policy applies to data collected during the download process.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies through third-party services (such as Google Analytics and Google AdSense). These cookies help us understand how visitors use our site. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

Children's Privacy

Our apps do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect personal data, our apps are safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
